About Our Career Academy

We are proud to say that Borde's has become a synonym of success for preparation of IBPS Bank PO, Clerk, SSC, & NDA exams. With a strong & experienced pool of faculties, the institute is committed itself to provide quality education & guideline for students & making them successful in different competitive & entrance exams preparation . With classroom programs, study material, online test series, Sankalp Education conducts regular test, quiz session & group discussion, which is very necessary for all over development of students.

Why Borde's?

At Borde's we believe in hard work not in just one's fate. All of us can have a good future if we work hard & honestly in the present moment. Surely success depends on the time & attention.
Here at Borde's, the vision of students is made very clear; what they want to achieve in their life & what they have to do. Time to time we conduct moral counseling also for students to get rid of many different types of personal & professional issues. We always boost up the students mentally & morally. The main philosophy at Borde's revolves around the facts & truth which result into a good outcome in the form of highly motivated, dedicated & laborious students who can think logically. We are always ready for any new challenges, which comes in our way to the mission.
