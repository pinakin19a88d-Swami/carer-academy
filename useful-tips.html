G.D AND P.I. TIPS

  • Be the first and also the Last to Speak,
  • Keep your cool and neither give one-word answers nor drone on in the GD and PI.
  • Learn to accept failure. Read, watch, listen!
  • Choose Magazines that are Rich in Content,Read newspapers and magazines on current issues,especially year-end issues that capture highlights of the year gone by. As well as read today/current News paper.
  • Watch and listen to the news and current affair programmes on news channels. As bbc London, Times Now,ND Tv
  • Think about a topic from five different prespectives -Social, Political, Economic, Legal And Technological, you would be able to generate a number of good points.
  • Your body language says a lot about you. Maintaining an eye contact, smiling faces are always welcomed.
  • Try to maintain a balanced tone in your discussion and analysis.
  • Don't lose your cool if anyone says anything you object to. Brush up on your leadership skills;
  • Motivate the other members of the team to speak, and listen to their views.
  • Be receptive to others' opinions and do not be abrasive or aggressive.
  • Be Aware of Topics that are Repeated Work on your Communication Skills Listen to the Topic Given During GD Carefully
